# Phase 1: Product Feedback & Discovery and Planning

This phase covers the initial stages of the SDLC from gathering product feedback through sprint planning.

## Overview

The Product Feedback & Discovery and Planning phase ensures that:
- Customer and stakeholder feedback is captured and prioritized
- Features are properly analyzed and estimated
- Sprint work is planned and committed

## Process Steps

| Step | Title | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | [Product Feedback](Step-01-Product-Feedback.md) | Capture and analyze product feedback from customers and stakeholders |
| 2 | [Change Request & Feature](Step-02-Change-Request-Feature.md) | Document change requests and new feature proposals |
| 3 | [Product Feature/Outcome Backlog](Step-03-Product-Feature-Outcome-Backlog.md) | Manage the product feature and outcome backlog |
| 4 | [Sprint/Kanban Backlog](Step-04-Sprint-Kanban-Backlog.md) | Organize work into sprint or Kanban backlogs |
| 5 | [Prioritization](Step-05-Prioritization.md) | Prioritize backlog items based on business value |
| 6 | [Backlog Refinement](Step-06-Backlog-Refinement.md) | Refine and detail backlog items |
| 7 | [Estimation](Step-07-Estimation.md) | Estimate effort for backlog items |
| 8 | [Sprint Commitment](Step-08-Sprint-Commitment.md) | Commit to sprint scope and goals |
| 9 | [Sprint Planning](Step-09-Sprint-Planning.md) | Plan sprint activities and assignments |

## Key Artifacts

- Product Backlog Items (PBIs)
- User Stories
- Sprint Backlogs
- Feature Documentation

## Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Product Owner | Owns backlog prioritization and feature decisions |
| Scrum Master | Facilitates ceremonies and removes impediments |
| Development Team | Provides estimates and commits to sprint work |
| Stakeholders | Provides feedback and requirements |

## Tools

- Azure DevOps Boards for backlog management
- Azure DevOps Queries for tracking
- Azure DevOps Dashboards for visibility
